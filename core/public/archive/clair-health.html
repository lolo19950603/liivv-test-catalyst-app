What is Clair?

Clair is a wearable continuous hormone monitor from Clair Health — the first of its kind. It reads your body's key signals (including estrogen, progesterone, LH, and FSH) without blood or urine, so you can see the shape of your cycle in real time. Pre-order it through Liivv.

When does Clair ship?

Clair is expected to ship around November 2026. You can pre-order through Liivv now so you're first in line — we'll keep you posted as dates firm up.

How does Clair work?

You wear Clair like jewellery. It continuously reads your body's key signals and shows the shape of your month — quiet weeks and loud ones — without pinpricks, blood, or urine strips. Check it like a glance at your wrist.

Do I need a lab visit or prescription?

No lab appointment is required to wear Clair. Pre-order through Liivv and we'll guide you through what's next as ship dates firm up.
